No Problem.

3DFYX calculates heading, pitch, and roll by measuring the displacement between each 3DFYX GPS puck on the airframe.

Heading

Heading is calculated by using GNSS positioning to determine the location of each 3DFYX puck to give a accurate heading without foward velocity.

Pitch

Pitch is calculated by measuring the slightest change in the y-axis between all the 3DFYX pucks.

Roll

3DFYX is configured at installation to identify with puck is where on the airframe allowing roll to also be messured by the y-axis displacement.
